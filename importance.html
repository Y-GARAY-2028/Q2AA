Is mental health important? How so?

Struggles

Mental health is essential for overall well-being, as it influences our thoughts, emotions, and behaviors, helping us handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices. Maintaining good mental health allows individuals to lead fulfilling lives, achieve their potential, and contribute positively to their communities.
